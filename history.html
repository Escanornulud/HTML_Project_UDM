CED History

The College of Education, formerly known as the Education Department, has a rich history dating back to its inception in 1995 as a premier college at the City College of Manila. Since its establishment, this pioneering program has been a stalwart in providing top-notch pre-service training, delivering curricula deeply rooted in Filipino values, and nurturing the future educators of our nation. Throughout its journey, the College has been under the able leadership of several distinguished individuals.

Prof. Francia G. Sierra served as the inaugural chairperson of the department from 1995 to 1997, laying the foundation for the College’s commitment to excellence in education. Subsequently, a succession of notable Deans continued to shape the College’s trajectory, including luminaries such as Dr. Ma. Rita R. Aranda, Dr. Susan S. Aguilos, Dr. Jose M. de Leon, Dr. Virginia I. Berganio, Dr. Ma. Jacqueline B. Patarata, Dr. Ma. Victoria I. Crisostomo, Dr. Antonio A. Taringting, and Prof. Alistair B. Selorio. Each of them contributed significantly to the College’s growth and mission.

However, on August 1, 2022, a new chapter in the history of the College of Education began with the appointment of Dr. Amalfi B. Tabin, Jr., a trailblazing faculty member and visionary educator, stepped into the role of Dean. His tenure heralds a fresh era for the College, marked by innovative developments and a steadfast commitment to excellence in education. With his extensive experience and forward-thinking approach, Dr. Tabin is poised to lead the College of Education toward new heights.

Under the dynamic leadership of Dr. Tabin, the College of Education is embarking on a transformative journey. This new phase is marked by a renewed emphasis on contemporary educational practices, cutting-edge research, and community engagement. Dr. Tabin’s vision includes fostering a dynamic learning environment that prepares educators to excel in the ever-evolving landscape of education. He is dedicated to equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to address the challenges of the 21st century.

In this exciting chapter of the College’s history, Dr. Amalfi B. Tabin, Jr. envisions a College of Education that not only maintains its commitment to Filipino values but also embraces innovation, diversity, and global perspectives. With his leadership, the College is set to make significant contributions to the field of education and produce educators who are not only well-prepared but also visionary in their approach.

As we look to the future, the College of Education, under the guidance of Dean Dr. Amalfi B. Tabin, Jr., stands ready to achieve new milestones and make a lasting impact on the landscape of education in the Philippines. The journey ahead promises to be one of growth, excellence, and unwavering dedication to the development of future educators and the betterment of our society.
