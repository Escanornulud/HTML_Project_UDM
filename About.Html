Project Rationale

This HTML website was created as a part of our required project in the course Technology for Teaching and Learning 2 (TTL 2). It serves as a digital representation of the College of Education at Universidad De Manila.

The website highlights various important sections such as the college’s Vision, Mission, Core Values, Course Offerings, Organizational Chart, and Activities. It was designed with both functionality and presentation in mind, utilizing basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create an engaging and interactive experience.

This project is being presented to Sir Ronnie Sta. Maria as a fulfillment of the final requirement for TTL 2 under the supervision of our instructor, Dr. Alona Timbre.

Our aim is to showcase the potential of integrating web development skills in the field of education, particularly in creating platforms that help in communication, information dissemination, and academic branding.
